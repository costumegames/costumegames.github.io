Sally Preisig, working under the company name of 'Mimics Productions Ltd.' has been a creative designer and maker of professional mascot costumes, puppets, soft props, masks and sets since 1988. Sally has also spent many years being the voice and character of some well loved children's TV characters.

In this workshop, Sally will show you how to use fur to maximum effect in creating your costume.

This workshop is free, but is unlikely to be over-subcribed. If you wish to be able to book a place at this session, you will need to purchase a VIP Festival pass, which will give you free entry into all of the ticketed events (except for the MOSHIMO Supper Club) and will allow you to book at any of the free events.
