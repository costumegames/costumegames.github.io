As part of our Sunday programme, Gladrags will be hosting a special Costume Carnival just for children. Come with your own costumes, a great idea or be inspired by the Gladrags team, children can then enter their own costume competition, with prize giving at 12 noon. Childrens outfits will be available to hire for as little as £8 from the Gladrags bus. More details coming soon!
