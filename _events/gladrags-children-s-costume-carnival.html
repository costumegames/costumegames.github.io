As part of our Sunday programme, Gladrags will be hosting a special Costume Carnival just for children. Come with your own costumes, a great idea or ask the Gladrags team to help, children can then enter the costume competition, with prize giving at 12 noon. Childrens costumes will be available to hire for as little as £8 from the Gladrags bus. More details coming soon!
