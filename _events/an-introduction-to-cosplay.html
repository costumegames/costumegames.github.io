Ever wondered about getting into Cosplay. This panel discussion is for you!

-What is Cosplay?
-How do you get into Cosplay?
-Disciplines of Cosplay (Wigs/Seamstressing/Prop-Armour/photography/Makeup.)
-Get Started! Our panel will quickly breakdown a basic costume, how to put it together and where to go.

Our Panel will be composed of :

Altria https://www.facebook.com/Altriacosplay Has been Cosplaying for over 9 years and a UK representative in international competitions, Altria specialises in Armour and Prosthetics. 

 

Anna S https://www.facebook.com/AnnaKS100 Anna has been cosplaying and photographing cosplayers for 5 years. She is a talented seamstress and core member of FNC https://www.facebook.com/FoodAndCosplay, and has attended cons around the world.

Nomes  https://www.facebook.com/NomesCosplay Nomes's has been a cosplayer for 5 years and has represented the UK in multiple international competitions. An all round costume maker, she specialises in complex wig styling.
