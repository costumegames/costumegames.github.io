Lady Aiko, an influential Japanese street artist resident in New York, will be coming over for The Costume Games to "Costume" the city's walls, care of Art Republic.

She will have a number of engagements and shows during her residency:

 

11 - 20th September/Ink'd Gallery

Ink'd show is titled 'Edo City Girl' and will feature stunning mixed media canvases in Lady Aiko's inimitable style where East meet West in a fusion of American urban pop and traditional Japanese aesthetics. There will also be an exclusive limited edition print release which will be hand finished LIVE at the opening night on Thursday 10th September 6.30-8.30pm. Please email gallery@ink-d.co.uk if you would like to attend. For more info visit http://www.ink-d.co.uk/whats-on"

16th September/Old Steine Gardens

Lady Aiko will be creating a large piece of art at the Old Steine Gardens, helping us to transform them into our home for The Costume Games over the following five days.
