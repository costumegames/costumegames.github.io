This one hour demo sees creativity explode which will inspire you to build incredible, high impact looks and effects as well as explore wild, outrageous make-up with extreme, exaggerated shapes. This masterclass also gives insight into Illamasqua’s Drag & Manga Make-Up Course, with Tonee demoing a taster on how to master pigments, brow blocking, statement brows, feature exaggeration, colour cartooning and lash effects.

After the masterclass, guests will also have a chance to chat one-to-one with Tonee.

If you would like to enrol onto Illamasqua’s Drag & Manga course, please visit their website here: http://www.illamasqua.com/shop/make-up-courses/drag-manga-make-up/. Alternatively, you can contact The School of Make-Up Art on 0203 4323 277.
