WELCOME TO THE COSTUME GAMES AWARDS

 

"Best Overall Costume”

We are looking for something truly stunning. Blow our judges away with your making skills and design flair. You will not be judged on accuracy but on the creativity of your craftsmanship. Be it Cosplay, LARP, Re-enactment, Steampunk, we’re inviting anyone who loves getting into character through costume to take part.

"Most Creative Costume”

This is your chance to let your imagination run wild. It doesn't need to be the most perfectly made costume, just show us who you are, whether it be a new character, a twist on an old favourite or something we could never have dreamt of." 

 

Prizes

Best Overall Costume

  • Brighton Museum will be opened exclusively for the winners to attend a private view of Christian Bale's Batsuit from The Dark Knight Rises, hosted by Graham Churchyard and Day Murch on the morning of Sunday 20th September.
  • 2 Nights Accommodation at Hotel Du Vin - including Breakfast
  • Dinner at Moshimo on Friday Evening / Or Lunch
  • Dinner at Terre é Terre on Saturday Evening / or Lunch
  • Visit To The Royal Pavilion
  • Visit to Sea Life Centre
  • Visit to Brighton Wheel
  • Theatre Royal Tickets for Saturday Evening ( Subject to programme )

Most Creative Costume

  • Brighton Museum will be opened exclusively for the winners to attend a private view of Christian Bale's Batsuit from The Dark Knight Rises, hosted by Graham Churchyard and Day Murch on the morning of Sunday 20th September.
  • Dinner at Terre é Terre on an Evening / or Lunch 
  • Visit To The Royal Pavilion
  • Visit to Sea Life Centre
  • Visit to Brighton Wheel 

Judges Pick - A prize for the costume with the most promise

  • Dinner at Moshimo on an Evening / or Lunch
  • Visit To The Royal Pavilion
  • Visit to Sea Life Centre

 

Members of the judging panel will be:-

Graham Churchyard - Costume Effects Supervisor (The Dark Knight/ Rises and Guardians of The Galaxy)

Vin Burnham - Costume designer and maker (Batman, The Fifth Element, Lady Gaga)

Day Murch – Costume Fabricator and the one and only 'Batsuit Wrangler'.

Adelaide Fillipe - Prosthetic Make-up Artist (Dracula Untold, 007-Spectre)
