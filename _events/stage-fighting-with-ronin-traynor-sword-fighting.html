This is a suberb opportunity to learn to stage fighting skills with one of the leading stage fighting directors in the country. Ronin Traynor has directed fights for over 300 productions including film, TV, musicals, opera, motion capture and numerous theatre shows in the UK and overseas, with credits ranging from Sega's Total War series, Kill Zone Mercenary (Playstation), Blue Peter (TV), feature films Candle to Water, The Powder Room, Gangs of Tooting Broadway;  series Chronicles of Syntax, Ren, The Syndicate and The First Musketeer. He's a certified teacher with the British Academy of Stage & Screen Combat and fencing coach with the British Academy of Fencing and Academie d'Armes Internationale. Clients include RADA, DSL, Pineapple Dance Studios, Independent Drama.

This workshop will be ticketed.
