We trawled the country high and low for the most outrageous tribute band we could find to turn our Costume Games Party on Saturday 19th into the party of the year… and we found them!

The Costume Games, in association with MOSHIMO, are proud to announce Liverpool’s extraordinary 70′s Glam Rock Tribute Show, KooKaChoo.

Koo Ka Choo have supported Slade, Alvin Stardust, Showaddywaddy, Tony Hadley, Dr & The Medics, Boney M and The Real Thing amongst others…and have themselves been supported by Liverpool Express and The Nolans.

10pm, 19th Saturday.

Free entry (as long as you are in costume!)

The Costume Games, Old Steine Gardens, Brighton
