Adelaide has worked as a prosthetics make-up artist on movies such as '007-Spectre', 'Pride and Prejudice' and 'Zombies/Dracula Untold'.

The demo will show you achievable ways of applying prosthetics that will help make your character complete. You will also have an opportunity to ask her questions after the demo.

www.adelaidefilippe.com

This workshop is free, but is highly likely to be over-subcribed. If you wish to be able to book a place at this session, you will need to purchase a VIP Festival pass, which will give you free entry into all of the ticketed events (except for the MOSHIMO Supper Club) and will allow you to book at any of the free events.
