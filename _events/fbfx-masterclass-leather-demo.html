Founded in 1993, FBFX has become the UK's leading suppliers of props and costume props to the film and entertainment industry, contributing to a huge number of major motion pictures and working with many of the worlds leading film directors and designers. For this one hour masterclass, they will be showing how they have worked with leather to create costumes on such films as Dark Knight Rises, Prometheus, War Horse and many, many others!

Please note: This workshop is free, but is highly likely to be over-subcribed. If you wish to be able to book a place at this session, you will need to purchase a VIP Festival pass, which will give you free entry into all of the ticketed events (except for the MOSHIMO Supper Club) and will allow you to book at any of the free events.
