There'll be prizes for the following:

Most Creative Costume    

Best Character Costume      

Best Onesie

Make your own Carnival Flag
Create your own epic Head-dress
Get the Carnival Beat from the Mistress of Rhythm

11.45 Gather for Parade  

12.00 Carnival Parade  

12.15 Prize Giving
