Brighton Japan is proud to present The Costume Games, a riotous, five day festival where the entire city of Brighton dresses up as its alter ego to celebrate the opening weekend of Rugby World Cup 2015.

The Costume Games are all about fun, creativity and participation, where everyone is invited to dress up and play.
